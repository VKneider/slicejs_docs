Route Guards

Overview

Route guards let you intercept navigation before and after a route change. Use them for auth, feature flags, redirects, analytics, and scroll or title updates.

Guards run on every navigation, including the initial page load. Call slice.router.start() after registering guards to ensure they are active before the first navigation.

Best Practices

Always call next() in beforeEach. Missing it logs a warning and navigation continues.
Keep guard logic fast. Long async work slows down navigation.

Gotchas

Guards can create redirect loops. The router detects loops and stops after 10 redirects.
afterEach cannot block navigation. Use beforeEach if you need to cancel or redirect.
